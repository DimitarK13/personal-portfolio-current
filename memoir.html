Embracing Challenges and Unraveling Innovation: The Journey of a Creative Problem Solver Eager to Learn

Hailing from the quaint city of Strumica in North Macedonia, my journey has been one of constant growth and exploration. At my core, I am a creative problem solver, unafraid of challenges and always eager to acquire new knowledge. Stepping out of my comfort zone is a natural inclination, as I strive daily to become the best version of myself. My passion for web development drives me forward, pushing the boundaries of what I can achieve. Beyond web development, I find solace in psychology books, thrive on strategic chess, and proudly support Barcelona football club.

My web development career started in 2020, and ever since it’s been a hell of a ride. Starting off with just HTML and CSS, I’ve always focused on details and how to properly use the languages and write code.

Even though I was avoiding JAVASCRIPT in the beginning, at some point I had to learn it, and now it is JAVASCRIPT that keeps web development fun for me. But my journey didn’t stop there.

After JAVASCRIPT, I continued with REACT, and tried a bit of back-end development with NODEJS and PHP, but quickly got back to front-end as I thought it suited me better, maybe I’ll reconsider, who knows.

Throughout my career, I tried opening a web development agency, but that didn’t go as planned (RIP Strax Designs 2021-2023) as I lacked the knowledge for both managing a business and development.

Apart from working on projects, I also devote my time to teaching web development to students at an academy in my city. I am passionate about sharing my knowledge and will continue to do so.
